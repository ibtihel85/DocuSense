DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)
Pursuant to Article 28 GDPR

This Data Processing Agreement is concluded between:

CONTROLLER: DataCorp AG, Bahnhofstrasse 10, 8001 Zurich ("Controller")
PROCESSOR: TechSolutions GmbH, Musterstraße 42, 80331 Munich ("Processor")

1. SUBJECT MATTER AND DURATION
The Processor processes personal data on behalf of the Controller for the purpose
of providing software services as defined in the Service Agreement dated January 1, 2024.
Duration of processing: same as the Service Agreement.

2. NATURE AND PURPOSE OF PROCESSING
- Types of data: Contact information, usage logs, authentication data
- Categories of data subjects: Employees and customers of the Controller
- Purpose: Software service delivery, support, and maintenance

3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE PROCESSOR (Art. 28 GDPR)
3.1 The Processor shall process personal data only on documented instructions
from the Controller.
3.2 The Processor ensures that persons authorised to process personal data
have committed to confidentiality.
3.3 The Processor implements appropriate technical and organisational measures
(Art. 32 GDPR):
  - AES-256 encryption at rest
  - TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  - Role-based access control
  - Regular penetration testing
  - ISO 27001 certification

4. SUB-PROCESSORS
The Processor shall not engage sub-processors without prior written authorisation
from the Controller. Current approved sub-processors are listed in Annex I.

5. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS
The Processor shall assist the Controller in fulfilling obligations regarding
data subject rights under Chapter III GDPR (access, rectification, erasure,
portability, restriction, objection).

6. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
The Processor shall notify the Controller without undue delay, and no later than
24 hours after becoming aware of a personal data breach.

7. DATA TRANSFERS
Personal data shall not be transferred outside the European Economic Area without
prior written consent of the Controller and appropriate transfer mechanisms
pursuant to Chapter V GDPR (Standard Contractual Clauses / adequacy decision).

8. DELETION AND RETURN
Upon termination of the DPA, the Processor shall delete or return all personal
data as instructed by the Controller within 30 days.

9. AUDIT RIGHTS
The Controller has the right to conduct audits or inspections to verify compliance
with this DPA. The Processor shall provide all necessary information.

Signed: January 1, 2024
